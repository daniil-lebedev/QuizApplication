Welcome to OpenTrain

OpenTrain is a cutting-edge, web-based application designed to transform how organizations conduct employee training. This platform is uniquely customizable and open-source, offering a new level of adaptability in corporate training environments.

Why Choose OpenTrain?

  • Customization: Tailor training modules to fit the specific needs of different industries, job roles, and employee skill levels.
  • Open-Source: Enhance and adapt your training environment continuously with the support of a community-driven platform.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Easy access to materials and efficient tracking of employee training progress.
  • Comprehensive Analytics: Measure the impact of your training programs with advanced analytics to continually improve educational outcomes.
